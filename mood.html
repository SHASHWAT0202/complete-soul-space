Emotional Wellness Categories

0%-30%: You're in a healthy emotional state.

31%-60%: You may be experiencing some stress. Consider self-care.

61%-90%: You're showing signs of depression. Seeking professional help is advised.

91%-100%: You're in a critical emotional state. Please consult a professional immediately.
