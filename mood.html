Assessment Criteria

The assessment is based on your responses to the questions above. Your emotions are categorized into:

  • Balanced/Stable: Positive responses with minimal distress.
  • Occasional Stress: Symptoms of mild emotional distress, such as anxiety or anger.
  • Emotional Struggles: Indicators of higher emotional distress, such as depression or chronic anxiety.
