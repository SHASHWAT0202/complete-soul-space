How to Perform Surya Namaskaram

  • Start in Pranamasana (Prayer Pose), standing with feet together and palms joined in front of your chest.
  • Inhale and raise your arms overhead into Hasta Uttanasana (Raised Arms Pose), arching your back slightly and looking up.
  • Exhale and fold forward into Padahastasana (Standing Forward Bend), bringing your hands to the floor next to your feet.
  • Inhale and step your right leg back into Ashwa Sanchalanasana (Equestrian Pose), placing your knee on the floor and lifting your chest forward.
  • Exhale and step your left leg back into Dandasana (Plank Pose), keeping your body in a straight line from head to heels.
  • Lower your knees, chest, and chin to the floor into Ashtanga Namaskara (Eight-Limbed Salutation), keeping your body in a straight line.
  • Inhale and lift your chest into Bhujangasana (Cobra Pose), extending your arms slightly and looking up.
  • Exhale and lift your hips towards the ceiling into Adho Mukha Svanasana (Downward Facing Dog Pose), forming an inverted V shape with your body.
  • Inhale and step your right foot forward into Ashwa Sanchalanasana (Equestrian Pose) again, bringing your left knee to the floor and lifting your chest forward.
  • Exhale and step your left foot forward into Padahastasana (Standing Forward Bend) again, bringing your forehead towards your knees.
  • Inhale and raise your arms into Hasta Uttanasana (Raised Arms Pose) again, arching your back slightly and looking up.
  • Exhale and bring your palms together in front of your chest, returning to Pranamasana (Prayer Pose).

Health Benefits of Surya Namaskaram

Improves Flexibility, Strength, and Balance

Regular practice enhances flexibility, strengthens muscles, and improves balance.

Boosts Metabolism

Stimulates the digestive system and helps with better metabolism.

Enhances Cardiovascular Health

Promotes better circulation, heart health, and overall cardiovascular function.

Improves Lung Capacity

Opens the chest and strengthens the lungs, improving breathing efficiency.

Reduces Stress and Tension

The flow of movement and breath calms the nervous system, reducing anxiety and stress.

Promotes Mental Clarity

Helps in sharpening focus and increasing mindfulness and concentration.

Boosts Energy Levels

Increases vitality and helps you feel more energized throughout the day.

Supports Weight Loss

Stimulates metabolism and aids in burning calories effectively.
